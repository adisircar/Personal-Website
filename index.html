Who Am I

I'm an engineer and business professional from Ashburn, Virginia.

Only a few years ago, the highlight of my life was eating my first steak. Since then, during my pursuit of a hero's life, I've found myself swimming in a freezing lake, zooming along an international motorcycle road trip, detained under the suspicion of murder, shaking hands with corrupt politicans, jet skiing in Dubai, dining on the KwaZulu-Natal coast, and playing street soccer in the heart of Mexico City.

This is my quest for excellence, truth, and immortality.
